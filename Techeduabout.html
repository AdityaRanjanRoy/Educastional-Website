About Techedu

Techedu is the complete online portal for technical students of West Bengal, which covers all the colleges under MAKAUT.

We believe to bring an educational transformation among all the technical students in West Bengal. We have a solution to every problem that a technical student faces during their academics. We at Techedu, are continuously helping technical students by providing them the best quality study resources based upon new carriculum also grooming them for Semester and Campus Interview. Our huge pool of study materials, suggestion papers, video lectures, previous year questions have not only helped students to get good grades in their semester, but also got placed during placement drives. We make learning easier and full of fun.

Our Mission

To provide end to end solution to technical students, to make smarter engineers by grooming them right from the beginning of their graduation, and ensuring a bright future for all the technical students.

Our Promises

We as a team Promises you to provide the best and the most recent resources along with latest trends of semester questions with updated routine of your upcoming semester. We also promise that all your doubts during the entire engineering life is going to be solved through Techedu.
